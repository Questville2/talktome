❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session? ▼
Simply click the "Book Now" button on our homepage, select your session type, choose a time, and complete the payment. You'll be matched with a listener instantly.
Is Talk to Me really anonymous? ▼
Yes! We never ask for your name, location, or any personal information. Your IP is hidden and we don't store logs of your calls or chats.
What if I'm in crisis right now? ▼
Talk to Me is not a crisis line. If you're in crisis, please call a professional helpline immediately. We've listed emergency numbers below for your convenience.
How do I become a listener? ▼
Click on "Become a Listener" on our homepage, fill out the application form, upload your documents, and pay the KES 2,000 annual membership fee. We'll review your application within 24-48 hours.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), M-Pesa, and bank transfers. All payments are securely processed.
